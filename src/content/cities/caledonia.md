## Caledonia's Housing Character

Caledonia is a charming small town with a genuine small-town character. The older residential streets near the Grand River feature century homes and mid-century bungalows — properties with original hardwood floors, plaster walls, and character details that reward thoughtful renovation. Restoration-minded owners often want to preserve original features while upgrading kitchens, bathrooms, and mechanical systems to modern standards.

Newer streets on Caledonia's growing edges have more recent builds — bungalows and two-storey homes from the 1990s and 2000s where unfinished basements represent the biggest untapped opportunity. These lower levels, properly finished, can add a bedroom, a bathroom, and significant livable square footage.
