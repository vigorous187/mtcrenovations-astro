## Cambridge Home Renovations

Cambridge is three distinct communities — Galt, Preston, and Hespeler — merged into one city, each with its own character and housing history. MTC Renovations serves homeowners throughout Cambridge with basement finishing, kitchen remodels, bathroom renovations, flooring installation, and professional painting.

## Three Communities, One Great Renovation Market

**Galt** is Cambridge's downtown core and most historic section. The Grand River gorge and the limestone commercial buildings give the area a character found nowhere else in Ontario. Residential streets near the downtown feature Victorian and Edwardian homes — properties with solid construction, high ceilings, and original details that benefit enormously from sensitively executed kitchen and bathroom renovations.

**Preston** has a more working-class postwar character with brick bungalows and side-splits built through the 1950s and 1960s. These properties are renovation-ready: kitchens that have never been updated since original construction, bathrooms with original tile and fixtures, and unfinished basements waiting to be converted to living space.

**Hespeler** on the Speed River is the most suburban of the three areas, with newer development mixing in with older village homes. The mill-era homes near the Speed are charming renovation candidates; the newer streets have more standard upgrade opportunities.
