## Winona Home Renovations by MTC

Winona sits quietly between Stoney Creek and Grimsby along the Lake Ontario shoreline — a community known for its fruit farms, greenhouses, and a growing mix of residential properties. MTC Renovations serves Winona homeowners with full-service basement finishing, kitchen renovations, bathroom remodels, flooring installation, and professional painting.

## Housing in Winona

Winona's housing stock is a blend of older bungalows and split-levels dating from the 1950s through 1970s, larger estate-style homes on rural lots, and newer subdivision homes built as the community has grown. The older bungalows and rural properties often present the most compelling renovation opportunities — layouts that can be opened up, outdated bathrooms that are ready for a full transformation, and basements that were never properly finished or waterproofed.

Newer Winona subdivision homes frequently need the same upgrades as comparable builds elsewhere in Stoney Creek and Grimsby: basement finishing, kitchen cabinet replacements, and bathroom tile work.
