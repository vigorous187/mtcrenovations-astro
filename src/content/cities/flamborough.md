Flamborough is one of Hamilton's most scenic and sought-after rural communities — stretching from Waterdown in the east to the border with Cambridge in the west, encompassing villages like Carlisle, Freelton, Millgrove, and Lynden. MTC Renovations serves homeowners throughout Flamborough with basement finishing, kitchen remodels, bathroom renovations, flooring, and professional painting.

## Renovating Flamborough's Distinctive Properties

Flamborough's housing stock is unlike any urban area we serve. Rural properties on acreage, stone farmhouses over a century old, newer estate homes on wooded lots, and village homes in Carlisle and Freelton — the variety is exceptional and each type demands a different renovation approach.

Stone and brick farmhouses are some of the most rewarding projects we take on. Original wide-plank floors, stone foundation walls, and century-old millwork create a backdrop for carefully designed modern kitchens and bathrooms that honour the character of the home. These projects take experience and patience — rushing a century home renovation shows quickly.

Newer estate builds in Flamborough's residential corridors are more straightforward, but homeowners here expect premium materials and execution. Walk-in closet builds, wet-bar basements, and spa-style primary bathrooms are common requests.
