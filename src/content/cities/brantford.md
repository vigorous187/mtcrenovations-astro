## Brantford's Housing Mix

Brantford has one of the most eclectic housing mixes in southwestern Ontario. The older core — neighbourhoods like Henderson Survey, Holmedale, and North Ward — is filled with Victorian-era and Edwardian homes loaded with character: original woodwork, stained glass, wide staircases, and high ceilings. Renovating these properties means balancing preservation with modernization, which is where an experienced crew makes all the difference.

Postwar bungalows dominate the west and east ends of the city — solid brick construction that responds very well to basement finishing and kitchen overhauls. Newer development in the north end and along the Highway 403 corridor brings contemporary builds where owners are upgrading quickly past the builder-grade starting point.
