## Grimsby's Unique Housing Stock

Grimsby is a town of contrasts. Along the lakeshore, you'll find a mix of historic summer cottages converted to year-round residences, bungalows from the postwar era, and a handful of century homes in the older core. These properties often need full electrical updates, new insulation, and modernized layouts before cosmetic renovation work begins.

Higher up near the Escarpment edge, newer subdivision homes in communities like Nelles Estates and Bartlett Place were built from the 2000s onward. These homes frequently need basement finishing, kitchen cabinet replacements, and bathroom tile upgrades to match the owners' growing investment in the property.

The ongoing residential development in Grimsby's south end brings more two-storey family homes where owners want to customize finishes beyond the builder-grade standard.
