## Mississauga's Housing Stock

Mississauga offers one of the most varied housing markets in the GTA. Along the lakefront in Port Credit and Lakeview, you'll find older bungalows and split-levels from the 1950s and 1960s that are ripe for full kitchen overhauls and bathroom modernization. Erin Mills and Churchill Meadows feature large two-storey homes from the 1990s where homeowners are now upgrading outdated builder-grade finishes with custom cabinetry, quartz countertops, and spa-inspired bathrooms.

Streetsville's charming century homes present unique challenges — plaster walls, knob-and-tube concerns, and mature layouts that require thoughtful planning before renovation begins. City Centre and Square One condo conversions call for precise, low-disruption work given building management requirements and shared-wall considerations.
