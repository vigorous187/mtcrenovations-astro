## New Floors, New Feel

Nothing transforms a room faster than new flooring. Whether you prefer the warmth of hardwood, the durability of luxury vinyl plank, or the elegance of tile, MTC Renovations delivers professional installation across Hamilton and the GTA.

## Our Flooring Services

We install all major flooring types including solid hardwood, engineered hardwood, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), ceramic tile, porcelain tile, and laminate. Every installation includes sub-floor inspection, preparation, and leveling as needed.

## Choosing the Right Floor

Hardwood suits main living areas and adds resale value. LVP handles moisture-prone areas like basements and kitchens. Tile is ideal for bathrooms and entryways. We help you pick the right material for each room.
