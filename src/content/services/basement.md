## Why Renovate Your Basement?

Your basement represents up to 40% of your home's total square footage. A finished basement adds living space, increases property value, and can generate rental income as a legal suite.

## What We Do

MTC Renovations handles every aspect of your basement renovation from start to finish. Whether you want a family room, home office, gym, or legal rental suite, we deliver high-quality results on time and on budget.

Our basement renovations include framing, insulation, electrical, plumbing rough-ins, drywall, flooring, painting, trim, and final finishes. We coordinate all trades so you have a single point of contact throughout the project.

## Hamilton Basement Renovation Costs

Basement renovation costs in Hamilton typically range from $35 to $75 per square foot depending on the scope and finishes. A standard 1,000 sq ft basement finishing runs $35,000 to $55,000, while a full legal suite with kitchen and bathroom can range from $60,000 to $90,000+.

We provide detailed, transparent quotes with no hidden fees. Every line item is visible so you know exactly where your money goes.
