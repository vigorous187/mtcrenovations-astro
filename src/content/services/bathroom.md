## Transform Your Bathroom

A bathroom renovation is one of the highest-ROI improvements you can make to your home. Whether you are updating a dated powder room or converting a tub into a walk-in shower, MTC Renovations delivers results you will love.

We handle complete bathroom renovations from demolition to final accessories. Our scope includes plumbing, electrical, waterproofing, tile, vanity installation, fixture mounting, and painting. Every trade is coordinated in-house.

Most standard bathroom renovations take 2 to 3 weeks. We protect your home during construction, clean up daily, and keep you updated on progress. Your bathroom renovation includes a detailed timeline so you know exactly what happens each day.
