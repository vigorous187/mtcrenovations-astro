## Fresh Paint, Fresh Start

A professional interior paint job is one of the fastest and most affordable ways to transform your home. MTC Renovations brings meticulous prep work, premium paints, and clean execution to every room.

## Our Painting Services

We handle complete interior painting including walls, ceilings, trim, baseboards, doors, and cabinets. Our scope includes drywall repair, patching, sanding, priming, and two coats of premium paint.

## Why Prep Matters

The difference between a DIY paint job and a professional result is in the preparation. We fill every nail hole, sand every patch, tape every edge, and prime every surface that needs it. The result is a flawless finish that lasts.
