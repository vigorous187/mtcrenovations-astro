Hamilton and broader Ontario homeowners often ask how to sequence decisions so budgets stay predictable. This guide reflects what we see on site—not a universal quote.

## Scope and budget drivers

Layout changes, plumbing moves, and finish selections swing totals more than small style tweaks. If you keep fixtures in place and focus on finishes, timelines compress and costs stabilize.

## Permits and inspections

When scope touches structure, HVAC, or plumbing stacks, permits may apply. We document inspections so resale stays clean.
